Patient Questionnaires

Please complete the form(s) requested by your doctor. Each questionnaire takes 5 to 15 minutes.

A note before you begin. Some of these questionnaires are long, and you may notice questions that feel similar to ones you've already answered. This is deliberate. The overlap helps your clinician build a more complete and reliable picture of your experiences.

Please answer as honestly and as fully as you can, but don't let any single difficult question stop you from submitting. If you find a question unclear, upsetting, or simply don't know the answer, it's fine to leave it blank or move on. A partially completed questionnaire is still genuinely useful. Please submit whatever you're able to.

Privacy & transparency notice

Privacy & Transparency Notice

Your data stays on your device

All responses you enter are processed entirely within your browser. Nothing is transmitted to any server while you are completing a questionnaire. Your answers are only sent to your clinician when you actively choose to download the results file and share it yourself.

Encrypted results file

When you download your results, they are encrypted before leaving your device. Only your clinician can decrypt and read the file. The results file contains only your responses and the information you enter (name, date of birth, completion date).

No AI analysis of your data

Your responses are not analysed, processed, or seen by any artificial intelligence system. The questionnaires themselves are established clinical instruments. AI tools were used to code and format them into this digital interface, but the content of what you submit is handled exclusively by your clinician.

Clinical confidentiality

Once received by your clinician, your results are subject to standard clinical confidentiality obligations. They form part of your clinical assessment and are not shared with third parties without your consent.

Questions

If you have any questions about how your information is used, please speak to your clinician directly.
